It's crucial you know what the property market has in store.

The Malta Real Estate Forum brings together leading independent minds from the island's property sector - and beyond - to give you concrete advice about the real estate market. So whether you're buying, renting, investing, or joining the industry yourself, you'll be able to make informed decisions about the future of property in Malta.

Reasons to attend Malta Real Estate Forum events:

  • Top speakers from across the real estate sector

  • Unbiased and informal panels - dedicated to giving you useful knowledge

  • In-depth information to help you gain insight about what the market will do next

  • The opportunity to ask experts your questions anonymously.
